Notary Service Guidelines

We are bonded and insured for your protection. Please follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth notary experience:

Prepare Identification

The signer must have valid picture ID.

Review Documents

Ensure all documents are read and understood before the appointment.

Wait to Sign

Do not sign documents prior to the notary’s arrival.

Signer's Condition

The signer must be alert, competent, and willing.

Witnesses

Arrange for any additional witnesses needed.

Documentation

All signings are recorded in a notary journal for reference.

Charge for Unprepared Signer

A $20 fee applies if the signer is unable or unwilling to sign.

Legal Advice

For legal questions or document preparation, consult an attorney.
